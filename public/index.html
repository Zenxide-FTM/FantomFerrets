THE STORY

The Ferrets are a phenomenally advanced species from the planet Boff, who explored their galaxy 6,000 years ago and colonized numerous planets all in the hopes of one day fulfilling the long-destined prophecy that one day a single ferret from a far-off land would conquer the known galaxy and be gifted powers to take over the universe. There are 12 planets in this galaxy that are inhabited by ferrets. Each planet and their respective societies are completely different. Most of all planets have abandoned their old ways and traditions. Most of all have forgotten about the great prophecy.

Now in the present day they are faced with war not with other species but, OTHER FERRETS! Several wanting their planet to be the central power of the galaxy have started attacking innocents on other planets in order to try and gain control. In order to prevent the death of billions of innocent ferrets, the planetary high council made out of 12 members (the respective king of each planet) agree upon a battle royal type competition where planet's kings are to pick their own competitors, choosing as many as they want and gifting them with a singular royal item to use in there fights to the death. These competitors with a royal item are given the title of Prince regardless of gender, age ,social status, or race.

The rules were simple.

After the complete deployment of all “Princes” the battle royal would begin structured like any other battle royal, Princes would fight to the death and the sole ferret left at the end would be deemed the winner of the battle royal. Every fortnight or 14 days, the planetary high council will gather and track how many princes still remain in the battle royal. Now the war is about to begin it is only a matter of time until we will see which ferret will be the CHAMPION!!!!!!!!!!
